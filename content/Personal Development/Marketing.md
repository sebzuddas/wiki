# Building an Audience
The purpose of marketing is to get a message to the right people. These people are your target audience. Different demographics can be found on different platforms - the average person using Instagram is different from the average person watching the TV. The first question to ask yourself when looking to build an audience is: "What are the goals of my audience?" - and try to answer that question as specifically as possible, asking a series of "why's" after you first answer it. This gives you a target audience to focus on. The second question to ask yourself is "what can I contribute to my audience trying to achieve that goal?" 

Whether marketing a product, or an idea, the core of it becomes making a clear link to how you are helping people achieve that goal. This is the value you deliver, and demonstrating to an audience how you deliver value is how you retain engagement or convert them.
#### Other Audience Building Concepts

- Signal => original ideas
- Create the content you want to see in the world
- The way you provide value to your audience is related to how you think & do things
- Volume without value is noise. Value + consistency is the ideal. 
- Others have to interact with your content, so you have to capture other people's content. 
- Leverage your authority - write about what others say, but state your authentic take. Don't blindly agree. This requires thinking, so the best way to act on it is to just start writing. 
- Create for your past, present and future self.
